About Me

Born and raised in Denver, CO. I began my early career working as a VIP hostess at a high volume nightclub while enrolled at CU Denver in International Business and Business Marketing. I quickly worked my way up the latter becoming the Marketing Director for Suite Two Hundred. I did that for 2 years before I was offered a promotion to open a new restaurant and nightclub, Chloe.

Opening Chloe was a wonderful expirience, and albeit a successful one! It was also during this expirience that I learned how to launch a business from start to finish and my entrepreneurial itch was scratched. I carried out my role at Chloe for 6 more months before launching my own boutique event staffing agency called, Serve the following year after completing a 12-week intensive Entrepreneurs Course.
